Interactive visualization tools and projects for exploring biological data.

## Visualization Projects

### TreeTangler
**[TreeTangler](https://github.com/dkbiocode/TreeTangler){:target="_blank"}**

A tool for visualizing and exploring phylogenetic trees and taxonomic relationships. TreeTangler helps untangle complex evolutionary relationships through interactive tree visualization.

### MA Plot Dashboard
**[dash-ma-plot](https://github.com/meekrob/dash-ma-plot){:target="_blank"}**

An interactive Dash application for creating and exploring MA plots (M-A scatter plots) commonly used in genomics and transcriptomics to visualize differential expression data. Features dynamic filtering and interactive exploration of gene expression changes.
